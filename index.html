about me

As a UX researcher and designer, I explore how people understand and engage with AI. My background in architecture, human–computer interaction, and immersive technology shapes a multidisciplinary approach to studying experience at the intersection of design, engineering, and intelligent systems. I focus on uncovering the social, cultural, and behavioral factors that influence how people adopt and interact with emerging technology.

My current research examines how designers and engineers form mental models of AI, particularly tools like ChatGPT, and how these models affect communication, decision-making, and collaboration. By analyzing prompting strategy, interaction dynamic, and adoption pattern, I develop frameworks that make human–AI interaction more intuitive, effective, and impactful. My goal is to guide the creation of user-centered, AI-driven solutions for the future.
